Beef Stew Recipe Steps and Ingredients

Steps of the Recipe

  • Gather ingredients:
    • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
    • 3 potatoes, diced
    • 4 carrots, sliced
    • 1 stalk celery, chopped

    Steps To Follow

    • Coat the beef cubes with flour, salt, and pepper.
    • In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the beef in hot oil over medium heat. Add garlic, bay leaf, paprika, Worcestershire sauce, onion, beef broth, potatoes, carrots, and celery.
    • Cover and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender. Remove bay leaf before serving.
