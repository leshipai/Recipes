Lasagna Recipe Steps and Ingredients.

Steps of the Recipe

  • The Ingredients as Follows
    • 9 lasagna noodles
    • 1 lb lean ground beef
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
    • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
    • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
    • 2 teaspoons white sugar
    • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 3/4 teaspoon salt
    • 3/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • The Steps as Follows:
    1. Cook lasagna noodles as per package instructions; drain and set aside.
    2. In a large skillet, cook and crumble beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
    3. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, sugar, basil, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    5. Remove and ready to serve.
